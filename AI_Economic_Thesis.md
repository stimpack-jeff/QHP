## The Axiom

**The marginal cost of reproducible cognitive labor is approaching zero.**

The cost of *generating* intelligence approaches zero. The cost of *validating* it does not. In healthcare — where errors hit patients, trigger enforcement, and create liability — budget for both.

This changes the economics of every company in the QHP portfolio.

---

## Two Consequences for Healthcare PE

### 1. Non-Collapsible Companies Win — If They Adopt Fast

When intelligence is cheap, companies built primarily on knowledge, analysis, or software lose their moats. Companies that operate in the physical world — manufacturing drugs, running clinical sites, handling biological materials — cannot be collapsed. Their intelligence costs dropped; their physical operations didn't.

QHP's portfolio is heavy on non-collapsible companies. The risk is not replacement by AI. The risk is that a competitor adopts AI faster.

**The test for every technology asset:** strip away the software. What's left? If the answer is proprietary data, regulatory status, and exclusive integrations — that's durable. If the answer is "a well-built platform that does X" — someone will rebuild it.

### 2. AI Advantage Evaporates Unless You Compound It

When intelligence goes to zero for you, it goes to zero for your competitors. If you cut costs 30% with AI, your competitor does too, and the market reprices.

Durable advantage requires deploying AI into live operations so it generates proprietary data — predictions scored against outcomes — that feeds back into the model continuously. A competitor who starts later can buy the same tools but cannot buy your accumulated operational data.

Every month a portfolio company delays AI deployment is a month of proprietary data it never accumulates.

---

## The Ask

1. **Deploy now.** The data flywheel only starts when AI is live. Start with shadow deployments where needed, full deployment where proven.
2. **Value what's inside, not what's built.** Data, regulatory status, and integrations are the moat. Code is not.
3. **Baseline everything.** Measure every process before AI touches it so we can quantify impact at exit.
4. **Budget for validation.** In regulated healthcare, the compliance layer is a real cost. Plan for it or the deployment stalls.
