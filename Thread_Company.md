# Thread

## Company Overview

Thread is a leading decentralized clinical trials (DCT) technology and consulting services provider that enables virtual research approaches to modernize clinical studies and registries.

## Basic Information

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Founded By** | Jeff Frazier |
| **Headquarters** | Cary, North Carolina (2000 Centregreen Way, Suite 300) |
| **Industry** | Clinical Research Technology |
| **Employees** | 200-500 |
| **Status** | Acquired (2019) |

## Company Description

Thread provides a comprehensive platform for decentralized and hybrid clinical trials, allowing patients to participate in research from home rather than requiring in-person site visits. The company pioneered many of the technologies now standard in modern clinical research.

## Technology Platform

### Core Offerings

1. **eCOA/ePRO** (Electronic Clinical Outcome Assessment / Electronic Patient-Reported Outcomes)
   - Patient-reported data capture
   - Validated instruments
   - Multi-device support

2. **eConsent**
   - Electronic informed consent
   - Remote consenting capabilities
   - Audit trail and compliance

3. **Telehealth Virtual Visits**
   - Remote patient engagement
   - Video visits with investigators
   - Virtual study visits

4. **Site Data Capture**
   - Electronic data capture for research sites
   - Integration with existing systems
   - Real-time data access

5. **Medical Device & Consumer Sensor Connection**
   - Wearable device integration
   - Connected medical devices
   - Continuous data collection

6. **Patient Engagement**
   - Reminders and notifications
   - Educational content
   - Adherence support

7. **Analytics**
   - Real-time DCT analytics
   - Benchmarking capabilities
   - Study performance dashboards

8. **Recruitment**
   - Digital patient recruitment tools
   - Targeting and optimization
   - Enrollment tracking

## Market Context

The decentralized clinical trials market has experienced explosive growth, accelerated by COVID-19. Key trends include:
- Hybrid trial designs (combination of in-person and virtual)
- Patient-centric approaches to research
- Regulatory acceptance of DCT methodologies
- Technology consolidation in the space

Thread positioned itself as a leader in this transformation, making it an attractive acquisition target and validating Jeff Frazier's vision and execution.
