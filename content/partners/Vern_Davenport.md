**Chairman & CEO, MedQuist/M*Modal** (~2010-2014)

This represents the signature achievement of Vern's career - a textbook case of business transformation:

**The Challenge:**
- MedQuist was a struggling medical transcription company
- Industry facing disruption from speech recognition technology
- Company needed complete strategic repositioning

**The Strategy:**
1. **Acquisition of M*Modal** - Acquired a speech technology company that would become the future of the business
2. **Complete Rebranding** - Renamed the entire company M*Modal to signal the strategic shift
3. **Take-Private Transaction** - Led the privatization to allow for transformation without public market scrutiny
4. **Technology Pivot** - Shifted focus from human transcription to AI-powered clinical documentation

**The Outcome:**
- M*Modal became a leader in clinical speech recognition
- Company was eventually acquired by 3M Healthcare for ~$1 billion (2019)
- Technology now powers clinical documentation across thousands of hospitals

**Leadership Lessons Demonstrated:**
- Vision to see technology disruption coming

- Execution discipline to manage complex transactions
- Patience to see transformation through
