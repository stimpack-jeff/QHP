- **GP Relationships**: Managing relationships with external private equity fund managers

**Sector Leadership:**

- Cross-geography mandate – invested across US markets and potentially international

**Strategic Context:**
During Jeff's tenure (early-to-mid 2010s), large pension funds like TRS were pioneering the move toward **direct/co-investment** to:
1. Reduce overall fee drag (avoiding the typical 2/20 structure on a portion of capital)

3. Build internal investment capabilities
4. Access larger deals with lower management fees

This gave Jeff a masterclass in:
