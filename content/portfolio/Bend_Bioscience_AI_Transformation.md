# Bend Bioscience: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Bend Bioscience** is QHP Capital's Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization (CDMO) specializing in small-molecule pharmaceuticals with end-to-end capabilities from preformulation through commercial manufacturing. Formed in 2024 through the combination of CoreRx, Societal CDMO, and Bend Bioscience, the company represents a comprehensive platform with three state-of-the-art U.S. facilities.

This document outlines a transformational AI strategy that positions Bend Bioscience as **the first AI-native CDMO in the pharmaceutical industry**—delivering **40% faster development timelines**, **35% better manufacturing yields**, and **industry-leading client satisfaction** through systematic AI deployment across formulation development, process optimization, quality systems, and client services.

### Product Innovation #1: AI-Powered Formulation Development Platform

**Problem:** Traditional formulation development is iterative trial-and-error, taking 12-18 months with 60-70% first-time success rate

**AI Solution:** Predictive formulation design platform that recommends optimal formulations upfront

#### Capability 1: Formulation Prediction AI

**Technical Approach:**
- Train ML models on Bend's historical formulation database (1000s of formulations across portfolio)
- Integrate physicochemical property databases (ChEMBL, PubChem)
- Incorporate biopharmaceutics classification system (BCS) data
- Use ensemble methods (random forests, gradient boosting, neural networks)

**Prediction Capabilities:**

| Prediction | Accuracy Target | Impact |
|------------|----------------|--------|
| **Optimal Dosage Form** | 85%+ | Right form, first time |
| **Excipient Selection** | 90%+ | Compatibility, performance |
| **Processing Method** | 85%+ | Direct compression vs. wet granulation vs. SDD |
| **Bioavailability** | 75%+ | Predict dissolution, absorption |
| **Stability (24 months)** | 80%+ | Fewer stability surprises |
| **Manufacturability** | 85%+ | Scalability prediction |

**Investment:** $800K (model development, data curation, validation)
**Timeline:** 18 months
**ROI:** **40% faster development** (18 mo → 11 mo) = $8-10M annual value

### Recommended Workforce Transition Approach

**Philosophy:** **Growth-oriented transformation**. Bend is expanding (new facilities, new capabilities). AI enables growth without proportional headcount growth.

#### Timeline & Approach

**Months 1-6: Foundation**
- Freeze hiring in reduction areas
- Deploy AI pilots (formulation, quality, manufacturing)
- Begin training programs
- Natural attrition: ~12-15 FTE (15% annual turnover)

**Months 7-12: Acceleration**
- Scale AI tools across operations
- Internal mobility (redeploy to growth areas, especially IT)
- Voluntary separation offers
- Expected reduction: 15-20 FTE (attrition + voluntary)

**Months 13-18: Optimization**
- Full AI deployment
- Organizational redesign
- Additional voluntary programs
- Expected reduction: 15-20 FTE

**Months 19-24: Stabilization**
- New operating model fully implemented
- Final adjustments through attrition
- Expected reduction: 5-10 FTE

#### Key Principle: **Attrition-Heavy Approach**

- **Target:** Achieve 50 FTE reduction with minimal involuntary separations
- **Natural attrition:** 15% annually in manufacturing/pharma = 80 FTE over 24 months
- **Challenge:** Hire selectively for growth areas (IT, client-facing) while reducing elsewhere
- **Result:** Net reduction achieved primarily through managed attrition + voluntary programs
