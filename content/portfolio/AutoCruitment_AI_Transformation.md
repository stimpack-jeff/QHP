# AutoCruitment: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**AutoCruitment** is QHP's digital patient recruitment platform company serving pharmaceutical sponsors, CROs, and research sites. This document outlines a comprehensive AI transformation strategy that could deliver **40-50% productivity improvement** while establishing AutoCruitment as the AI-powered leader in clinical trial patient recruitment.

### Key Opportunity

Patient recruitment represents 30-40% of total clinical trial costs and is the #1 cause of trial delays. AutoCruitment's current manual processes for ad creation, targeting, patient matching, and campaign optimization present massive AI opportunity. By deploying sophisticated AI across advertising, patient engagement, and analytics, AutoCruitment can dramatically improve conversion rates while reducing cost-per-randomization.

### Business Model

AutoCruitment operates on a **performance-based** model:

1. **Fee per Pre-Screened Patient** - $200-400 per qualified lead
2. **Fee per Randomized Patient** - $2,000-3,000 per enrolled patient
3. **Hybrid Models** - Combination of monthly retainer + performance fees
4. **Platform Licensing** - Some clients license technology directly

**Key Economics:**
- Gross margin: ~60-70% (digital advertising model)
- Operating margin: ~25-35% (labor-intensive campaign management)
- Customer acquisition cost: High (pharma sales cycles 6-12 months)

#### Product 3: **MatchIQ™** (AI Patient-Study Matching Engine)

**Current Problem:**
- 8-12% of pre-screened patients ultimately randomize
- Major drop-off due to poor initial matching

**AI Solution:**

| Matching Factor | Current | AI-Enhanced |

| **Site Capacity** | Manual tracking | Real-time prediction |

**Technical Approach:**
- **Feature Engineering:** 50+ features per patient-study pair

- **Training Data:** Historical data on 13,700+ enrollments (success/failure)
- **Real-Time Scoring:** API returns match score for each patient-study combination
- **Continuous Learning:** Model retrains monthly on new data

**Business Impact:**
- Pre-screen to randomization rate: 8-12% → 18-25%
- Cost per randomization: -40% (fewer wasted pre-screens)
- Site satisfaction: +50% (higher quality referrals)
- Patient satisfaction: +40% (better matches)

| **Talent acquisition (ML engineers)** | Medium | Medium | Competitive comp; equity; interesting problem domain; remote work options |

### Specific Risk: HIPAA Compliance

**Challenge:** Patient data is protected health information (PHI)

**Mitigation:**
1. **Infrastructure:** AWS/Azure with HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
2. **Data Minimization:** Only use de-identified data for model training where possible
3. **Encryption:** Data encrypted at rest and in transit
4. **Access Controls:** Role-based access; audit logs
5. **Vendor Management:** All AI vendors (Anthropic, OpenAI) must be HIPAA-compliant
6. **Legal Review:** HIPAA counsel review of all AI implementations

**Investment:** $100K (security enhancements, legal review)
