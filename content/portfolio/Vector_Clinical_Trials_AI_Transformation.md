# Vector Clinical Trials: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Vector Clinical Trials** is QHP's community-focused clinical trial site network operating in the Western U.S., partnering with community doctors to recruit diverse patient populations for clinical research. This document outlines a comprehensive AI transformation strategy that could yield **40-50% improvement in patient enrollment efficiency**, **25-30% reduction in operational costs**, and position Vector as the most technologically advanced community-focused site network in the country.

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Current State | Post-Transformation (24 mo) |

| **Patient Enrollment Rate** | Industry average | +40-50% improvement |
| **Screen Failure Rate** | ~30-40% | 15-20% |
| **Site Utilization** | Baseline | +35% improvement |
| **Data Quality Metrics** | Standard | 50% fewer queries |
| **Patient Retention** | Industry average | 90%+ retention |
