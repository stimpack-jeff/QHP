# Applied StemCell: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Applied StemCell (ASC)** is QHP's genome engineering platform company providing gene editing, iPSC services, and cGMP manufacturing for the cell and gene therapy industry. This document outlines a comprehensive AI transformation strategy that could deliver **40-50% productivity improvement** across R&D, manufacturing, and client services while establishing ASC as the AI-native leader in the precision medicine CDMO space.

### Key Transformation Metrics

| Metric | Current State | Post-Transformation (24 mo) |
|--------|---------------|----------------------------|
| **Experiment Design Time** | 2-4 weeks manual | 3-5 days AI-assisted |
| **Cell Line Development Success Rate** | 60-70% (industry avg) | 85-90% AI-predicted |
