# Catalyst Clinical Research: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Catalyst Clinical Research** is QHP Capital's full-service, technology-enabled, oncology-focused Contract Research Organization (CRO). With its **Catalyst Oncology** and **Catalyst Flex** (FSP) brands, Catalyst serves biotech and biopharma companies globally across early and late-phase clinical trials. This document outlines a comprehensive AI transformation strategy that could yield **35-45% productivity improvement** while positioning Catalyst as the most AI-enabled oncology CRO in the market.

**Critical Context:** Catalyst is being acquired by Worldwide Clinical Trials, which deepens QHP's potential value realization and makes AI transformation even more strategic for demonstrating enterprise value.
