# COPILOT Provider Support Services: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**COPILOT Provider Support Services** is QHP's hub services company connecting patients with specialty medications through benefits verification, prior authorization, patient support, and adherence programs. This document outlines a comprehensive AI transformation strategy that could deliver **50-60% productivity improvement** in core processes while establishing COPILOT as the AI-powered leader in pharma hub services.

### Key Opportunity

Hub services are **extraordinarily labor-intensive**: benefits verification requires hours of manual phone calls and portal navigation, prior authorizations involve extensive form-filling and documentation, and patient support demands 24/7 human availability. These processes are ripe for AI automation. By deploying sophisticated AI across benefits verification, prior authorization, patient engagement, and payer intelligence, COPILOT can dramatically improve turnaround times while reducing cost-per-case.

## Product & Services Layer: AI-Enhanced Offerings

### Current State: Labor-Intensive Manual Processes

The hub services industry is one of healthcare's most **manual, labor-intensive** segments:

**Benefits Verification Process (Current State):**
1. **Receive request** from prescriber/pharmacy (phone, fax, portal)
2. **Manual research** - Call payer, navigate portal (30-60 min per case)
3. **Document benefits** - Medical vs. pharmacy, coverage details
4. **Identify PA requirements** - Manual policy review
5. **Patient outreach** - Verify demographics, consent
6. **Compile report** - Manual data entry and documentation
7. **Send to stakeholders** - Prescriber, pharmacy, patient

**Total Time:** 45-90 minutes per BV
**Cost:** $40-60 per BV (mostly labor)
**Error Rate:** 5-10% (manual data entry errors)

**Prior Authorization Process (Current State):**
1. **Clinical documentation gathering** - Request from prescriber (multiple calls/faxes)
2. **Form completion** - Navigate payer portal, fill forms (45-90 min)
3. **Supporting documentation** - Compile chart notes, labs, prior treatments
4. **Submission** - Fax, portal upload, or ePA (if available)
5. **Follow-up** - Track status, call payer (multiple times)
6. **Appeals** (if denied) - Additional documentation, peer-to-peer setup
7. **Notification** - Inform all parties of outcome

**Total Time:** 2-4 hours per PA (more if appealed)
**Cost:** $150-300 per PA
**Approval Rate:** 75-80% (first submission)
**Timeline:** 3-7 days average, up to 14+ days

#### Product 2: **COPILOT AuthorizeAI™** (Intelligent Prior Authorization)

**Description:** AI-powered prior authorization platform that doubles approval rates on first submission and reduces TAT by 60%.

**AI Capabilities:**

##### 1. **Clinical Criteria Matching**

**Problem:** PA denials often occur because submissions don't address payer criteria

**AI Solution:**
- Maintain RAG database of every payer's medical policies (10,000+ policies)
- AI reads prescriber documentation and extracts clinical facts
- ML model scores patient match against payer criteria
- AI generates "gap analysis" - what's missing for approval

**Technical Approach:**
```
Patient Chart + Rx → Claude extracts clinical facts
                  ↓
Payer Policy Database (vector embeddings) ← Semantic search
                  ↓
ML Scoring Model → Match probability: 85%
                  ↓
Gap Analysis: "Missing: 2 prior failed therapies documented"
                  ↓
AI Recommendation: "Request prescriber document tramadol trial (dates)"
```

**Impact:** First-approval rate: 75-80% → 88-92%

---

##### 2. **Intelligent Form Filling**

**Problem:** PA forms are payer-specific, 5-15 pages, require precise clinical language

**AI Solution:**
- AI reads prescriber notes (handwritten, typed, dictated)
- Extracts relevant clinical information
- Auto-fills PA forms with appropriate clinical language
- Flags missing information for follow-up

**Technical Stack:**
- Computer vision (Textract, Google Vision) for handwriting
- NLP (Claude) for clinical note summarization
- Template mapping engine (custom)
- Form automation (RPA)

**Impact:** Form completion time: 45-90 min → 10-15 min (review/refinement)

### Specific Risk: HIPAA Compliance with AI

**Challenge:** Using AI (cloud-based LLMs) with protected health information (PHI)

**Comprehensive Mitigation:**

1. **Infrastructure:**
   - AWS/Azure HIPAA-eligible services only
   - Business Associate Agreements (BAA) with all vendors
   - COPILOT already HITRUST e1 certified (strong foundation)

2. **AI Vendor Selection:**
   - **Anthropic (Claude):** HIPAA-compliant via AWS Bedrock
   - **OpenAI (GPT-4):** Azure OpenAI Service (BAA available)
   - All vendors must sign BAAs

3. **Data Handling:**
   - **De-identification** where possible for model training
   - **Encryption** at rest and in transit (AES-256)
   - **Access controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
   - **Audit logging:** Comprehensive logs of all PHI access

4. **Data Minimization:**
   - AI models only receive minimum necessary PHI
   - Anonymization/pseudonymization where feasible
   - No PHI stored in logs

5. **Governance:**
   - Privacy Impact Assessments for each AI system
   - Regular security audits
   - Incident response plan
   - Staff training on AI + HIPAA

**Investment:** $150K (security enhancements, legal review, audits)
**Outcome:** HIPAA-compliant AI architecture, defensible in audit
