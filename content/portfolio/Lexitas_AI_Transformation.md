# Lexitas Pharma Services: AI Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Lexitas Pharma Services** is QHP Capital's specialized ophthalmology-focused Contract Research Organization (CRO), providing full-service clinical trial support exclusively for eye disease treatments. With 14+ years dedicated to ophthalmology, Lexitas serves biopharma and medical device companies across all ophthalmic subspecialties. This document outlines a comprehensive AI transformation strategy that could yield **35-50% productivity improvement** while positioning Lexitas as the most technologically advanced ophthalmology CRO globally.
