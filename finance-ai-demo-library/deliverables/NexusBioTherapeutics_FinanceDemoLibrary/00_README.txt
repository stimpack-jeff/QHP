==============================================================
Version: 1.0 | Generated: March 2026 | For Workshop Use Only

ABOUT THIS LIBRARY
------------------
This is a comprehensive library of 853+ simulated financial source documents
for a fictional mid-size life sciences company, Nexus BioTherapeutics, Inc.
(NASDAQ: NXTB). All documents are internally consistent — the same vendors,
employees, GL accounts, trial names, and product details appear throughout.

The library is designed to demonstrate AI-powered finance transformation:
how AI can ingest, classify, extract data from, and process the full range
of documents that flow through a real pharma finance organization.

DOCUMENT CATEGORIES
-------------------
01_AP/  — Accounts Payable (vendor invoices, POs, packing slips, wire confirmations)
02_AR/  — Accounts Receivable (customer invoices, aging, remittances, chargebacks)
03_TravelExpense/ — T&E (hotel folios, restaurant receipts, airline, rideshare, expense reports)
04_Payroll/ — Payroll (bi-weekly registers, Excel files)
05_Banking_Treasury/ — Bank statements (3 accounts x 12 months), wire confirmations
06_GL_Accounting/ — Journal entries, GL detail reports, trial balances
07_FinancialStatements/ — Monthly P&L, quarterly cash flow, budget vs actual
08_ClinicalTrials_RnD/ — CRO invoices (3 active trials), CMO manufacturing invoices
09_FaxDocuments/ — Inbound fax cover sheets (scanned JPEG)
10_Other/ — Legal invoices, insurance premiums, misc scanned receipts
