INTELLIGENCE FRAMEWORK ANSWER KEY (for facilitator use — do not distribute)

  1.  FULL AI      — Computational. Pure data extraction at scale.
  2.  FULL AI      — Computational + Reasoning. Cross-referencing structured data.
  3.  FULL AI      — Computational. Rules-based entry generation.
  4.  AI + HUMAN   — AI flags it (Reasoning). Human decides: void / contact vendor /
                     escalate. That's Judgment — choosing from a menu of options.
  5.  AI + HUMAN   — AI drafts the narrative (Reasoning + Creative). Human edits
                     for voice, emphasis, and strategic framing.
  6.  HUMAN + AI   — AI surfaces the data (Reasoning). The decision requires
                     Wisdom — questioning whether the trial's strategic frame is
                     still correct. No menu of predefined options captures this.
  7.  FULL AI      — Predictive + Reasoning. Pattern-based forecasting within
                     a defined model.
  8.  HUMAN ONLY   — Pure Wisdom. Questioning whether the entire CRO relationship
                     frame is right. AI has no basis for this assessment.
  9.  FULL AI      — Reasoning. Policy application across structured line items.
                     AI catches it faster and more consistently than humans.
  10. HUMAN + AI   — AI identifies the violation (Reasoning). The enforcement
                     decision requires Judgment at minimum (choose: enforce /
                     waive / counsel). But the deeper question — "Is our policy
                     even written clearly enough?" — is Wisdom.
