# Nexus Program Brief — NXT-301

- Phase III randomized study in relapsed non-Hodgkin lymphoma
- Primary endpoint: progression-free survival
- Key secondary endpoints: ORR, duration of response, patient-reported symptom burden
- Imaging adjudication currently handled centrally, but site imaging windows vary by up to 10 days
- PRO collection missingness was 14% in the last operational review, concentrated in EU sites
- Investor relations deck currently references symptom burden improvement as a differentiating point
