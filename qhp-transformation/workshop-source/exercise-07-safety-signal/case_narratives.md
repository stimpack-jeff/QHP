# Selected Case Narratives

## AE-112-021
Patient P-024 developed AST 5.4x ULN and ALT 3.8x ULN on Cycle 1 Day 15. No baseline hepatic impairment. Concomitant medication included trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole started five days before the event.

## AE-112-028
Patient P-031 presented with fatigue, nausea, and bilirubin 2.2x ULN on Cycle 2 Day 3. Investigator assessed event as related given temporal association after second dosing cycle. Viral hepatitis panel negative.

## AE-112-041
Patient P-044 had ALT 6.1x ULN on routine monitoring with no symptoms. Event occurred after protocol-defined intra-patient dose escalation. Imaging showed no new hepatic metastases.
