# Prior Safety Review Committee Excerpt

The committee previously agreed that isolated Grade 2 transaminase elevations would remain expected background noise unless one of the following occurred:
- two or more Grade 3 hepatic events in a rolling 30-day period
- bilirubin increase suggesting Hy's law concern
- clear clustering after dose escalation

Action threshold if triggered:
- prepare expedited aggregate review
- consider temporary hold on further dose escalation
- request blinded review of concomitant medication confounders
