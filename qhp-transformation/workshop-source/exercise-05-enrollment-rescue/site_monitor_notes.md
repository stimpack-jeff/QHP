# Site Monitor Notes — April 2026

## Germany / Spain cluster
- Investigators report repeated confusion over prior-therapy exclusion wording after protocol amendment v4.2.
- Local coordinators are escalating pathology turnaround delays for fresh tumor samples.
- Two candidate patients were lost after re-consenting pushed visits into holiday closures.

## US community referral pattern
- Digital campaign is generating names quickly, but pre-screen quality is poor.
- Referring offices often send patients before washout and lab timing are confirmed.
- Duke and Mass General both asked for a shorter pre-screen checklist to reduce coordinator back-and-forth.

## Canada / UK
- Performance is below plan but comparatively stable.
- Teams believe a dedicated pathology courier workflow could reduce missed sample windows.
