INTELLIGENCE FRAMEWORK ANSWER KEY (for facilitator use — do not distribute)

   1.  FULL AI              — Computational. Pure data extraction at scale.
   2.  FULL AI              — Computational + Reasoning. Cross-referencing
                                           structured data.
   3.  FULL AI              — Computational. Rules-based entry generation.
   4.  AI + HUMAN REVIEW    — AI flags it (Reasoning). Human reviews the case and
                                           decides: void / contact vendor / escalate. That's
                                           Judgment — choosing from a menu of options.
   5.  AI + HUMAN REVIEW    — AI drafts the narrative (Reasoning + Creative).
                                           Human edits for voice, emphasis, and strategic framing.
   6.  HUMAN ACCOUNTABILITY — AI surfaces the data, scenarios, and tradeoffs.
                                           The decision reaches Wisdom because it may require
                                           questioning whether the trial's strategic frame is
                                           still correct, but AI can still help pressure-test it.
   7.  FULL AI              — Predictive + Reasoning. Pattern-based forecasting
                                           within a defined model.
   8.  HUMAN ACCOUNTABILITY — This is Wisdom-heavy. AI can challenge the frame,
                                           surface alternative paths, and summarize second-order
                                           effects, but leadership still owns the strategic choice.
   9.  FULL AI              — Reasoning. Policy application across structured line
                                           items. AI catches it faster and more consistently.
   10. HUMAN ACCOUNTABILITY — AI identifies the violation (Reasoning), suggests
                                           options, and can even question whether the policy is
                                           written well. The final enforcement consequence still
                                           belongs to a human manager.
