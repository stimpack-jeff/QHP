## Your Assignment Framework

For each portfolio company assigned to you, create a comprehensive AI transformation strategy document following the **Clinical Ink template** (see reference file: `/Portfolio_Companies/Clinical_Ink_AI_Transformation.md`).

### Required Sections for Each Company

#### 1. Executive Summary
- Company overview (what they do, size, market position)
- Key AI transformation opportunity
- Headline metrics (current state → future state)

#### 2. Company Deep Dive
- Leadership team (research their executives)
- Products/services detailed breakdown
- Competitive landscape
- Current technology stack (if discoverable)
- Recent news, partnerships, acquisitions

#### 3. Strategic Layer: Executive AI Applications
For each C-suite role, identify:
- Current responsibilities
- AI tools that would enhance their effectiveness
- Build vs. Buy recommendations
- Specific use cases

#### 4. Product & Services Layer: AI-Enhanced Offerings
- How can AI transform their core products/services?
- New AI-native product opportunities
- Competitive differentiation through AI
- Specific technical approaches (architecture diagrams where helpful)

#### 5. Operations Layer: Department-by-Department Transformation
For each major department, analyze:
- Current state (estimated headcount, processes)
- AI transformation opportunities
- Automation rates by process
- **Organizational impact including potential headcount changes**
- Workforce transition recommendations

**Be direct about layoffs where appropriate.** Jeff wants real transformation analysis, not sugar-coated recommendations. If AI can reduce a department by 50%, say so.

#### 6. Organizational Restructuring Summary
- Headcount impact table by department
- Net workforce transformation diagram
- Recommended approach (attrition, retraining, reduction)
- Timeline for workforce changes

#### 7. Financial Impact Model
- Investment required (Year 1, Year 2)
- Returns (labor savings, revenue growth, margin improvement)
- ROI calculation
- Payback period

#### 8. Implementation Roadmap
- Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Foundation
- Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Acceleration
- Phase 3 (Months 13-24): Transformation

#### 9. Risk Mitigation
- Key risks and mitigation strategies

#### 10. QHP Cross-Portfolio Synergies
- How does this company connect to other QHP portfolio companies?
- What AI playbooks can be transferred?
